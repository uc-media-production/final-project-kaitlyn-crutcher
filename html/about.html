About Us

We started selling golf carts in 2016 when my dad and I started refubishing old carts. Since then we have became an authorized dealer of Bintelli Golf Carts as well as Icon EV and Epic Carts and have grown into a 2,000 sqft showroom. We still customize old carts to your liking and put our heart and soul into these carts to give our customers the best quality and service we can offer.
